CHAI CHAYEI

When it comes to food, prospello do believe that feeding hunger is one of the most divine services that anyone can do. We have a lot of dreams when it comes to serving people with the most delicious and happening foods to ensure that we do satisfy their minds along with their tummy. Prospello have laid its foundation in launching chai chayei from 2022, a traditional kerala tea outlet to be spread all across the world uplifting the taste and love from the kitchen of prospello.

HEU VOYAGE

Being passionate travel lovers, we here at Prospello do believe that the worlds best medicine to stress is travel and the best doctor is nature, that's when the idea of heu voyage was born. We couldn't find any better idea than stepping into the leisure industry when it came to spreading happiness and peace to the customers. HEU STORE

Prospello have started its initial discussions and research on this project expected to be launched by 2023
